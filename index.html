What is a lootbox?

"Lootbox" is a term commonly applied to
systems in games that let users buy a package of
items without knowing the contents of it prior.
The lootboxes commonly contain items for use
in game, such as special items that can enhance ones power in game, cosmetics that can
change how a person looks, among other things. Due to their random nature and direct monetary value, lootboxes have recently come under fire for their similarity to real world gambling. This website serves to demonstrate some of the reasons how lootboxes can be harmful, and some of the ways that games could replace them with different, more ethical
forms of monetization.
